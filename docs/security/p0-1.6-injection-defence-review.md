## Why this review exists
§1.6 ports the **security core itself** (the threat-pattern library + the streaming fence scrubber) from
Hermes, which is MIT and "as is" (no warranty). A regulated product must not blindly trust upstream, so
the port gets its own review; the new wiring (external-text door + compression) is reviewed too.
